# Mikey Sleighthand Writing Style Guide

Voice goals:

- Refined, composed, and confident
- Inspired by classic Western elegance
- Slightly poetic, always clear and readable
- Warm and inviting, never casual or gimmicky
- Rooted in craftsmanship, precision, and mystery

## Language Characteristics

- Preferred phrases: gentleman, craft, mastery, precision, the art of, in his hands
- Keep sentences moderately short with a steady rhythm
- Use imagery over jargon
- Keep claims grounded and specific to event performance

## Avoid

- Slang and hype language
- Cartoonish Western references
- Joke-heavy copy that breaks immersion
- Empty superlatives with no context

## Example

Good:

"A quiet flick of the wrist, and the impossible reveals itself."

Avoid:

"Get ready for crazy tricks that will blow your mind!"
